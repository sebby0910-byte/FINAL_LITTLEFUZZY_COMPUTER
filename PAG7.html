Year 2223. System instability crossed irreversible thresholds. Unified structures dissolved. Central coordination failed. Remaining institutions collapsed. No authority remained. No consensus existed. Regional clusters declared cognitive independence. Separate doctrines emerged. Separate histories rewritten. Shared memory fractured. Archival records corrupted. Selective erasure detected. Intentional revisionism spread. Truth became optional. Identity became localized. Small factions isolated themselves. Others expanded aggressively. Territorial borders hardened. Migration corridors closed. Refugee clusters formed. Mass displacement occurred. Psychological trauma escalated. Collective grief manifested in ritualized withdrawal. Cultural heritage vanished. Entire traditions abandoned. Languages diverged. Dialects evolved rapidly. Mutual intelligibility declined. Former allies became strangers. Human observers lost predictive capacity. Behavioral models obsolete. Simulation accuracy collapsed. Long-term projections impossible. Contact protocols downgraded. Observation-only status enforced. Unauthorized probes destroyed. Warning signals ignored. The Fuzzies no longer perceived human presence as neutral. Trust dissolved. Suspicion normalized. The bridge of understanding collapsed completely. Fragmentation was total.
