Year 2222. Divergent systems began to destabilize rapidly. Feedback loops collapsed. Information networks overloaded. Misinformation cascaded. Contradictory narratives multiplied. Consensus became impossible. Faction leaders lost influence. Radical subgroups emerged. Extreme interpretations spread. Behavioral volatility increased. Collective anxiety peaked. Episodes of mass withdrawal were recorded. Entire clusters ceased communication. Others escalated signaling to destructive levels. Resource zones were contested. Ritual spaces were vandalized. Symbolic attacks occurred. Cultural infrastructure failed. Emergency mediation attempted. All efforts ineffective. Human intervention was reconsidered. Risk thresholds exceeded. Containment scenarios evaluated. Direct contact rejected. Too dangerous. Too late. Unauthorized transmissions spiked. Signal origins traced to internal actors. Proto-authoritarian structures detected. Control networks formed. Information monopolies emerged. Dissent suppressed. Cognitive diversity declined. Creativity collapsed. Adaptability weakened. System resilience failed. Environmental disruptions amplified stress. Minor climate shifts triggered panic. Migration surges destabilized regions. Population density reached critical levels. Predictive models projected civilizational breakdown within five cycles. Humanity observed a society approaching collapse. And knew it had helped create it.
